R2A medium

 R2A is generally purchased as a premade dehydrated media.

Recipe for R2A medium

  • 0.5 g yeast extract
  • 0.5 g protease peptone (Difco no. 3)
  • 0.5 g casamino acids
  • 0.5 g glucose
  • 0.5 g starch
  • 0.3 g pyruvate
  • 0.3 g K2HPO4
  • 0.05 g MgSO4 x 7-H2O
  • 15.0 g agar
  • 1000 mL nano-pure H2O
  • Autoclave at 121 Â°C and 15 psi for 30 min.
