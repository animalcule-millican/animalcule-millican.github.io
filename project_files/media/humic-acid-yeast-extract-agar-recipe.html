Humic acid yeast extract agar

Variation of humic acid agar which uses yeast extract in place of vitamin solution

Recipe:

  1. In 998 mL of nanopure H2O add:
    • 1g humid acid
    • 1.7g KCl (potassium chloride)
    • 0.5g Na2HPO4 (SUBSTITUE: K2HPO4)
    • 0.5g MgSO4 (magnesium sulfate)
    • 0.02g CaCO3 (calcium carbonate)
    • 0.01g FeSO4 (ferric sulfate)
    • 15g agar (can be 15g)
  2. 1g yeast extract
  3. Autoclave for 30 min at 121^o^C and 15 psi
  4. Pour plates
