BG11 trace metal solution recipe

Trace metal solution for BG11 and BG11-SE media.

Trace metal mix:

  • Add 980 ml of nano-pure H2O to a 1 L bottle
  • Add the following to the bottle:
    • 2.9 g H3BO3
    • 1.81 g MnCl2 x 4 H2O
    • 0.22 g ZnSO4 x 7 H2O
    • 0.39 g Na2MoO4 x 2 H2O
    • 10 ml CuSO4 x 5 H2O (0.8 g/100 ml stock solution)
    • 10 ml Co(NO3)2 x 6 H2O (0.5 g/100 ml stock solution)
