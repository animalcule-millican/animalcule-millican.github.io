Isolation of cyanobacteria from soil

media preparation

[[Cyanobacteria-Medium-BG11]] [[Cyanobacteria-Medium-Soil-enriched]] * Note:

isolation procedure:

  • dry soil for 1 week at 25C in dark
  • After drying, soak dried soil with sterile nanopure H2O, allow to sit for 1 day.
  • After soaking, add nanopure H2O to cover soil, place in shaker at 150 rpm, leave on shaker for 2 hrs
  • Make 3 - 10-fold dilutions, then spread 100ul of each dilution onto AGAR plates.
  • Place under continuous light at 22-30C.
  • Incubate for up to 1 month.
  • Evaluate and note growth daily.

  • After incubation period, streak colonies for isolation.

  • Identification of pure cultures can be achieved by Cyanobacterial specific primers CYA-359F (5′-GGGGAATYTTCCGCAATGGG-3′) and CYA-781R (5′-GACTACWGGGGTATCTAATCCCW-3′) @Nubel1997 @Lee2017 ‌
