Minimal salt media

Minimal media amended with ACC (ACC-MM)

  • Prepare organism specific minimal media without a nitrogen source
  • Amend media with 5mM ACC as sole nitrogen source

Minimal media (MM)

Inoculum preparation

  • Organism is cultured in organism specific minimal media for 48h.
    • Culturing can go longer if needed
  • Culture is centrifuged at 5000rpm for 10 min.
  • Supernatant is then discarded
  • Pellet is resuspended in 1mM phosphate buffer

  • Steps 2-4 are repeated three times.

    Note: - For organisms that do not grow in liquid media, growth on solid media can be substituted. - After incubation collect culture from plate with swab and resuspend in 1mM phosphate buffer - Then proceed with washing +++

    ACC assay

    • 1 mL of washed microbial suspension was added to 4 mL of ACC-MM
    • Incubate culture for ~3 days at 22C
    • After incubation centrifuge culture and resuspend in 1 mL 0.1 M Tris-HCl buffer (pH 8.5)
    • Add 100 uL of 0.5M ACC and 1 mL Tris-buffer.
    • Incubate at room temperature for 30 mins with shaking at 150 rpm.
    • After incubation, add:
    • 150 uL 0.2% 2,4-dinitrophenylhydrazine reagent (in 2 N HCl) and 690 uL 0.56 N HCl
    • Incubate for 30 min at room temperature
    • Then add 1 mL 2N NaOH
    • Observe and record color
    • Change in color from yellow to brown is regarded as positive for ACC deaminase production.
