Siderophore production assay protocol

  1. Inoculate a single isolated colony to isolate appropriate liquid rich media.
  2. Incubate for 48 h at 25^o^C with shaking.
  3. After incubation, remove 0.5 ml from culture and place into micro-centrifuge tube
  4. Centrifuge culture for 5 mins at full speed
  5. Remove 100 ul and place in a well of a micro-titer plate
  6. Add 100 ul CAS reagent
  7. Incubate in dark for 1 h
  8. After incubation, measure optical density of micro-titer plates at 630nm.
  9. Siderophore production is calculated as: Siderophore = ((blank - Abs)*100)/blank
