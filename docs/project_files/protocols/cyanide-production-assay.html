Hydrogen Cyanide production assay

  • Overnight culture supplemented with glycine (4.4g/L)
  • Attach filter paper to underside of Petri dish lid
  • Flood filter paper with 0.5% picric acid in 2% sodium carbonate
  • Incubate for 7 days at 28C
  • After incubation observe color of filter paper
    • change from yellow to orange/red indicates HCN production @33
