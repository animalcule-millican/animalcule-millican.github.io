Cellulase activity assay

  1. Prepare isolate appropriate minimal defined media with the addition of 1% carboxy methyl cellulose and 0.1 % of yeast extract.
  2. Inoculate a single isolated colony to isolate appropriate liquid rich media.
  3. Incubate for 48 h at 25^o^C with shaking.
  4. After incubation adjust culture to OD~600nm~ 0.1
  5. Place 4-5 ul drops of adjusted culture in four quadrants of PKA plate, spaced equidistant from each other.
    • NOTE: Unless unavoidable, do not inoculate different isolates onto the same plate.
  6. Incubate at 28^o^C for up to 5 days
    • Incubation timing is dependent on isolate growth rate
  7. After incubation, flood plates with 0.01% Congo red and stain for 15 min.
  8. Decant Congo red solution and flood plate with 1% NaCl and de-stain for 5 min
  9. Observe for clearing zones around colonies
  10. Measure and record diameter of clearing zones
