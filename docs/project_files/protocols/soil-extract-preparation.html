Soil extract preparation

Step 1:

  • Place ~ 300 g soil into a 1 L flask or bottle.
    • Ideally, use soil from the environment in which you will be isolating microbes.
  • Add 700 ml DI-H~2~O to container.
  • Cover container with cap or foil.
  • Autoclave for 1 hour at 121^o^C;.
  • Allow to sit at room temperature for 24 hours.

Step 2:

  • After 24 hours, repeat autoclaving for 1 hour at 121^o^C.
  • Allow to sit overnight undisturbed.

Step 3:

  • Filter mixture through a Whatman #1 filter to remove particles.
    • Do not disturbed larger particles that have settled overnight.
  • Filter sterilize (PES membrane, pore size 0.22 μm).
