IAA production assay

  1. Inoculate a single isolated colony to isolate appropriate 1/10 diluted rich medium supplemented with 0.5% L-tryptophan.
  2. Incubate up to 5 days at 25^o^C with shaking.
  3. After incubation, removed 1 mL of bacterial culture and transfer to sterile micro-centrifuge tube.
  4. Centrifuge tubes for 2 min at full speed (~10,000xg) in a table-top centrifuge.
  5. Remove supernate, being careful not to remove bacteria from pellet, and transfer to fresh test tube.
  6. Add 2 mL Salkowski’s reagent
  7. Incubate solution for 30 min at room temperature in the dark.
  8. After incubation, measure optical density at 530 nm.

IAA standard curve

  1. prepare 5 solutions of 10, 20, 50, 100, and 150 ug/ml IAA
  2. Mix 1 ml of each solution with 2 mL Salkowski’s reagent
  3. Incubate for 30 min at room temperature in the dark.
  4. After incubation measure optical density at 530 nm.
