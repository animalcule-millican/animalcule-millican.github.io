Pikovskaya's Agar

  • This media is generally available as a premade powder.

Step 1

  • Fill a 2L flask or bottle with 1000 ml DI-H~2~O
  • Weigh out ingredients and add to water in container
    • Yeast extract - 0.5 g
    • Dextrose - 10.0 g
    • Tri-calcium phosphate - 5.0 g
    • Ammonium sulfate - 0.5 g
    • Potassium chloride - 0.2 g
    • Magnesium sulfate - 0.1 g
    • Manganese sulfate - 0.0001 g
    • Ferrous sulfate - 0.0001 g

Step 2

  • Loosely screw on cap to bottle
    • or cover opening of flask with aluminum foil
  • Mark with autoclave tape
  • Autoclave media at 121^o^C and 15 psi for 25 mins
  • After autoclaving allow media to cool to ~ 65^o^C
  • Dispense 15 ml media into 100 mm petri plates
  • Cover plates and allow to dry overnight
  • Package plates into bags and tape closed
  • Place on shelf to store for later
