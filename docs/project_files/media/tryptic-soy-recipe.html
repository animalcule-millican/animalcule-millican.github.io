Tryptic soy agar

 TSA is generally purchased as a premade dehydrated media.

Direction of making in lab:

  • Add 1000 mL of nano-pure H2O a 2 L flask
  • To the flask add:
    • 15 g Casein peptone
    • 5 g soy peptone
    • 5 g NaCl
    • 15 g agar
  • Autoclave at 121 Â°C and 15 psi for 30 min.

Note: for broth media, omit addition of agar
