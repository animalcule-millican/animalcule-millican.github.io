Cyano ES medium micronutrient solution

- Micronutrient solution for the construction of BG11 media to isolate cyanobacteria

Micronutrient solution

- 50    ml  **Micronutrients Component 1**  
- 950   ml  **Micronutrients Component 2**
  1. For Micronutrient solution combine Component 1 and Component 2 and adjust to 1 l
  2. Freeze aliquots for storage

Micronutrients Component 1

- 0.7   g   FeSO4 x 7 H2O   
- 2 g   Na-EDTA x 2 H2O
  1. Combine both solutions (yields a clear solution)

Micronutrients Component 2

  • 1 ml ZnSO4 x 7 H2O (1 g/L stock solution)
  • 2 ml MnSO4 x H2O (0.76 g/L stock solution)
  • 5 ml H3BO3 (2 g/L stock solution)
  • 0.2 ml Co(NO3)2 x 6 H2O (5 g/L stock solution)
  • 5 ml Na2MoO4 x 2 H2O (0.2 g/L stock solution)
  • 0.02 ml CuSO4 x 5 H2O (0.25 g/L stock solution)
  • 0.4 g Na-EDTA x 2 H2O
