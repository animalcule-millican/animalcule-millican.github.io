Soil extract agar

Preparation of soil extract

  • The following make 1 L of soil extract, this can be adjusted down if 1 L is too much.

Choosing soil

  • Choose soil the same or similar to soil you are sampling from.
  • The soil should not contain too much humus or clay
  • Avoid soil treated with fertilizers, herbicides or fungicides

Making soil extract

  • place ~400 g of soil into a 2 L flask
  • Add add 700 mL of distilled H2O
  • Shake vigorously
  • Autoclave for 1 h at 121 °C and let cool down
  • After 24 h autoclave for a second time (for 1 h at 121 °C)
  • Remove bigger particles by filtering through cheesecloth
  • Filter through Whatman filter 595 (1/2, 320 mm) to remove residual particles
  • Filter sterilize
  • Store at 4°C – 10°C
