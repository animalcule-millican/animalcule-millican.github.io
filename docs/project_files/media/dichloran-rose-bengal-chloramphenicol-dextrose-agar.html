Dichloran Rose-bengal Chloramphenicol Dextrose agar

  • DRBC agar can be purchased as a premade powder
  • For the isolation of fast growing filamentous fungi and yeast.

  • Enriches the isolation of Fusarium and Trichoderma fungi from soil environments.

    ** Note: DRBC broth can be constructed by omitting the agar **

Recipe:

  1. Add 998 ml DI-H~2~O to a 2L flask or bottle
  2. Add the following components to the DI-H~2~O:
    • Add 5 g peptone no 3
    • Add 10 g dextrose
    • Add 1.0 g KH2PO4
    • Add 0.5 g MgSO4
    • Add 2.0 mg Dichloran
    • Add 25 mg Rose Bengal
    • Add 0.1 g Chloramphenicol
    • Add 15.0 g agar
  3. Autoclave at 121 Â°C and 15 psi for 30 min.
  4. Pour plates
